Brian Eckmann

UX/UI Design

Solving problems has always been the best way to engage my mind. In college, I discovered I had an aptitude to solve IT problems. The cybersecurity industry was taking off, and I started my career as an IT security consultant. While the left side of my brain was challenged with the work, the right side was bored. I wanted to build something and my creative appetite was growing.

As I looked for new challenges to fuel my creative interests, I kept running into a user experience problem when sending mobile content to family and friends. I got excited about building an app to solve the problem, but I had one major roadblock: I didn’t know how to design it. After some basic wireframing and requirement gathering something sparked. I really enjoy this, maybe I should be a UX designer.

Shortly thereafter, I immersed myself in the User Experience Design boot camp offered by BLOC. I worked with an industry professional over Skype to guide me through the course as a teacher, client, and mentor. As a graduate of the program, I’ve gained the fundamental user experience design skills, coupled with my experience as an IT consultant, to give me what I need to help clients achieve their goals.

This is only the first chapter of my story. I’m excited to learn and grow within the user experience design world and build things people will enjoy!
